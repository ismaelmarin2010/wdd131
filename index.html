Cordoba, Argentina

Cordoba is the 2nd largest city in Argentina, and it is located at the heart of our country. It is the home of the famous National University of Córdoba, founded in 1613 by the Jesuits. It is one of the oldest universities in the Americas and helped shape Argentina’s intellectual history.
